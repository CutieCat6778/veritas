Veritas Privacy & Content Policy

Effective date: January 6, 2025

At Veritas, developed by Thinh Nguyen, we are committed to respecting your privacy and the intellectual property rights of content creators. This policy outlines how we handle content and your information.

1. Content Usage

  • Veritas only uses publicly available RSS feeds.
  • Displays titles, short summaries, publisher name, and publication date.
  • Full articles or content behind paywalls are not stored or displayed.
  • Every article links back to the original publisher, respecting all copyright and intellectual property rights.

2. Non-Commercial Use

Veritas is completely free.

  • The app does not display ads, charge subscriptions, or offer in-app purchases.
  • Redistribution of full content or commercial use of feed content is not permitted.

3. Privacy

Veritas does not collect personal data from users.

  • No tracking, analytics, or third-party data collection occurs in the app.
  • Your reading preferences and habits remain entirely on your device.
  • We do not share any information with third parties because we don't collect any.

4. User Responsibility

  • Users can add RSS feeds that are publicly available.
  • Users must ensure that any feeds they add comply with the original publisher's terms of use.
  • Veritas is a tool for personal use and should not be used to redistribute copyrighted content.

5. Content Rights

All content displayed in Veritas remains the intellectual property of the original publishers. Veritas acts solely as an aggregator and linking service. We:

  • Do not alter, modify, or republish original content
  • Always provide attribution to the original source
  • Direct users to the publisher's website for full articles
  • Will remove any feed immediately upon request from the publisher

6. Publisher Permissions

If you are a publisher and have concerns about your content being aggregated through Veritas, please contact us. We respect all takedown requests and will respond promptly.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy and content policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
