About

Veritas, created by Thinh Nguyen, is a neutral RSS-based news aggregator for iOS. It collects publicly available news feeds from reputable publishers and presents them in a clean, easy-to-read format.

Experience news aggregation without the noise, ads, or distractions. Veritas brings you the information you need, when you need it.

How It Works

  • Displays headlines, publisher name, publication date, and short summaries from RSS feeds.
  • Provides direct links to the original articles.
  • Ensures that all content rights remain with the original publishers.
  • Offers a clutter-free experience with no ads, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

App Preview

Our Mission

Veritas is designed to help readers stay informed without altering or reproducing full articles. It is not a replacement for the original news websites but a convenient tool to navigate multiple sources from a single app.

We believe in transparency, neutrality, and respect for original content creators. Every article you read through Veritas supports the publishers who create it.
